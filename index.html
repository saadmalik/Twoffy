Schedule your tweets on the
fly with a quick email.

Simple & Sweet

We've made the process as clean and simple as possible. Just type in our email address in the "To:" field, your personalized identification number (for security purposes) in the "Subject:" field, and your tweet in the body. Click send and you're done!

Automatically Shortens Tweet

Don't worry if your tweet is longer than 140 characters! Twoffy will automatically shorten your tweet down to 140 characters and place a URL at the end. This URL will be linked to a page where your full tweet can be viewed.

Multiple Twitter Accounts

It's a pain to constantly sign in and out when you're managing more than one Twitter account. Twoffy allows you to use multiple accounts at the same time to schedule your tweets.
